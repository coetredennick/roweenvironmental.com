We Are Friends of the Water

At Rowe Environmental Services, we believe in preserving the natural beauty and health of aquatic ecosystems through mechanical removal—never chemicals. Our specialized equipment and expertise ensure your waterways remain safe for fish, wildlife, pets, and irrigation while restoring clarity and usability.

What We Do

Our Services

Aquatic Weed Removal

Mechanical removal of invasive vegetation including hydrilla, water hyacinth, duckweed, and algae. Root extraction ensures lasting results without harming aquatic life.

  • Safe for fish & wildlife
  • No chemical contamination
  • Immediate water access

Shoreline Restoration

Complete shoreline clearing and restoration to reclaim access to your waterfront. Debris removal, vegetation management, and erosion control.

  • Access restoration
  • Debris haul-away
  • Erosion prevention

Irrigation Canal Clearing

Professional canal and intake clearing to maintain consistent water flow. Protect pumps and irrigation systems from vegetation damage.

  • Flow optimization
  • Pump protection
  • System efficiency

Sediment Removal

Light dredging and selective sediment extraction to restore depth and improve water quality. Compliant disposal with minimal environmental impact.

  • Depth restoration
  • Water quality improvement
  • Compliant disposal
